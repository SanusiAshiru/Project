  2. Nassau

    3. With its stunning beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture, Nassau is a must-visit destination in the Bahamas. Nestled on the island of New Providence, Nassau offers a unique blend of tropical paradise and urban charm.

  3. Exuma Cays Land and Sea Park

    4. This stretch of pristine water, land and beaches is an ecological preserve and wildlife refuge famous for its breathtaking marine environment.Scuba diving and snorkeling are popular here, and you can encounter nature firsthand by feeding the endangered Bahamian iguanas or catching a glimpse of the elusive hutia, with the assistance of one of the professional guides.
